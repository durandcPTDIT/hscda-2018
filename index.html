Holy Spirit Child Development Academy

The Holy Spirit Child Development Academy is an infant and toddler child care facility serving babies from 6 weeks to children through three years of age. Our mission is to provide a nurturing, Christ centered, environment to foster the development of our children. We are accredited through the Florida Catholic Conference and have Religious Exempt status through the Department of Children and Families, License number: X01ES0063.

OUR MISSION

The Holy Spirit Child Development Academy provides a safe and nurturing environment for the development of children and families in our community through Christ-centered childcare and preschool programs in the long-standing tradition of Catholic education excellence.
